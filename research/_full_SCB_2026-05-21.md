## 3. การดำเนินธุรกิจในอนาคต

### 3.1 SCB X strategy = "regional digital financial ecosystem"

**Vision:** ไม่ใช่ traditional bank — เป็น **financial super-app + venture portfolio**

3 layers:
1. **Core banking** (SCB Bank) — stable cash flow engine
2. **Adjacent fintech** (CardX, AutoX, AISCB, Monix) — growth + scale
3. **Frontier ventures** (Token X, DataX, Purple Ventures) — optionality

### 3.2 4 strategic pillars

**Pillar 1 — Wealth + HNW Banking**
- Thai HNW market ~150,000 คน
- Fee margin 1-2% AUM (high vs NIM 2.5%)
- SCB Wealth Premier — best positioning ใน Thai market
- Target: AUM 1T+ by 2030

**Pillar 2 — Digital lending + payments**
- CardX, AutoX, AISCB, Monix
- Lower customer acquisition cost vs traditional bank
- Higher NIM 5-10% (vs traditional 2.5%)
- Risk: digital lending NPL cycle untested

**Pillar 3 — Capital markets + IB**
- Innovest X = investment banking + securities
- Thai IPO market underwriting leader
- M&A advisory
- Wealth management synergy

**Pillar 4 — Digital ecosystem + ventures**
- Robinhood (failed 2024 — ปิด) = lesson learned
- Token X = crypto/digital asset
- DataX + SCB Tech X = AI/big data
- Purple Ventures = VC bet

### 3.3 Rate cycle exposure (different from BBL)

- SCB core NII ~60-65% (vs BBL ~75%)
- Non-NII higher ~35-40% (vs BBL ~25%)
- → **rate cycle impact น้อยกว่า BBL ประมาณ 30%**
- 2026-2027 rate cut = NII drag 5% (vs BBL 8-10%)

### 3.4 Restructure risks + benefits

**Benefits:**
- Capital flexibility cross-subsidiary
- Tax optimization
- Strategic clarity per business unit
- Spinoff option future (เช่น CardX IPO)

**Risks:**
- Governance complexity (13 boards)
- Cross-subsidiary related-party transactions
- Performance fragmentation
- Cost duplication (multiple HR/IT)

### 3.5 Long-term thesis 10-20 ปี

**Bull narrative:** SCB X กลายเป็น "regional digital bank leader" — fintech ecosystem ที่ disrupt traditional banking → re-rate PE 10 → 15+
**Base narrative:** Mixed performance — core bank slow + selected fintech ที่ work + venture failure mixed → moderate growth
**Bear narrative:** Restructure ไม่ deliver value + traditional bank revenue compress + venture losses → underperform

→ SCB = **"transformation play"** ไม่ใช่ pure bank — high optionality
