## Buffett Block 25% — 4 ตัว Thai quality compounders

**Criteria ที่ Buffett ใช้:**
1. **Moat:** competitive advantage ที่ลอกเลียนไม่ได้
2. **ROE high + stable** (>15% sustained)
3. **EPS CAGR 12-20% long-term**
4. **Pricing power** (ขึ้นราคาได้ตามเงินเฟ้อ)
5. **Reasonable price** (PE ไม่เกิน 25-30 สำหรับ quality)
6. **Hold forever** mentality

**Thai market quality compounder candidates** (historically delivered 10-30 bagger):

| Sym | Sector | Weight | Yield | Moat | EPS CAGR 10y est | Why |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|---:|---|
| **CPALL** | Consumer Retail | 8% | 2.5% | 7-Eleven monopoly Thailand (14,000+ stores) + MAKRO + Lotus's acquisition | ~12-15% | Defensive consumer, daily traffic, scale moat |
| **BDMS** | Healthcare | 7% | 1.8% | Largest private hospital network ASEAN (60+ hospitals: Bangkok Hospital, Samitivej, BNH) | ~10-13% | Aging demographic + medical tourism + consolidation |
| **AOT** | Infrastructure | 5% | 1.5% | Airport monopoly Thailand (6 international + Don Mueang) — concession ถึง 2050s | ~12-18% (recovery + tourism) | Travel secular + duty-free royalty |
| **HMPRO** | DIY Retail | 5% | 3.5% | HomePro DIY chain Thailand (130+ stores) + MegaHome + market share 60%+ | ~8-12% | DIY trend + home improvement secular |
| **Total** | | **25%** | weighted ~2.2% | | | |

**Sector breakdown ใน Buffett block:**
- Consumer Retail: CPALL 8% + HMPRO 5% = 13%
- Healthcare: BDMS 7%
- Infrastructure: AOT 5%

**Why these specifically:**

**CPALL (C.P. All)** — 7-Eleven monopoly
- Monopoly defensive consumer (daily customer traffic)
- Acquired MAKRO 2013 + Lotus's 2020 = retail ecosystem complete
- E-commerce + 24/7 store + delivery integration
- **Moat:** scale + location density + brand 30+ ปี
- **Caveat:** PE 25-30 ตอนนี้ ไม่ใช่ "cheap" — Buffett-ish but premium

**BDMS (Bangkok Dusit Medical Services)**
- Largest private hospital network ในไทย (60+ hospitals)
- Brand: Bangkok Hospital, Samitivej, BNH, Phyathai, Paolo Memorial
- Medical tourism gateway (Middle East patients fly in)
- Aging Thai demographic + ASEAN middle class growth
- **Moat:** doctor network + brand + capital intensity barrier to entry
- **Caveat:** Capex heavy + government healthcare policy risk

**AOT (Airports of Thailand)**
- 6 international airports (Suvarnabhumi, Don Mueang, Phuket, Chiang Mai, Chiang Rai, Hat Yai)
- Concession ถึง 2050s — irreplaceable
- Recovery from COVID + secular tourism rebound (Chinese return)
- Duty-free royalty (King Power) + retail rental + airline landing fee
- **Moat:** government-granted monopoly + capital intensity infinite
- **Caveat:** State-controlled (Ministry of Finance ~70%) — pricing decision political

**HMPRO (Home Product Center)**
- HomePro DIY chain 130+ stores + MegaHome + Mega.com (online)
- DIY/home improvement trend Thailand
- Market share 60%+ (Boonthavorn ที่ 2 ห่างไกล)
- **Moat:** store network density + brand + supplier relationship
- **Caveat:** Property cycle exposure (housing market slow = DIY slow)

## Risk Management Rules

### Position sizing limits (already built in)
- Single name max: 12% (BBL/AMATA)
- Single sector max: 25% (Banking 22% เกือบเต็ม)
- Single block max: 75% (Niwes 70%, room flex)

### Rebalancing rules
- **Quarterly review** — ดู ranking ใหม่จาก /scan
- **Rebalance trigger:** sector exposure deviate >±5% from target
- **Sell signals:** DPS cut + business thesis broken + Tier downgrade S→C
- **Buy signals:** เพิ่ม weight ตัวที่ Tier upgrade + valuation drop

### Exit / pause rules per block
- **Niwes:** PBV >1.0 (deep value gone) → pause new buy; DPS cut 2 ปีติด → reassess
- **Buffett:** PE >30 (overpriced) → pause; ROE drop <10% 2y → reassess
- **Cash:** rebuild to 5% if deployed

### Tax efficiency
- **DCA monthly** — spread cost basis
- **Hold long-term** — Thai market ไม่มี capital gain tax สำหรับ SET stocks (เกือบทั้งหมด)
- **Dividend tax:** withholding 10% ที่จ่ายแล้ว (สามารถเลือก credit ภาษีคืนได้)

## Caveat สำคัญที่ user ต้องเข้าใจ

1. **ตัวเลข return เป็น expected value, ไม่ใช่ guarantee**
   - Bear case 5y: Niwes 30%, Buffett 50% total return (~5-9% annualized)
   - Base case 5y: Niwes 80%, Buffett 130% (~12-18% annualized)
   - Bull case 5y: Niwes 110%, Buffett 200%+ (~16-25% annualized)
   - **Expected ~14-16% blended** = base case scenario

2. **ตัวที่ confidence ★ (ILM, AEONTS) ต้อง DD เพิ่มก่อนใส่เต็ม weight**
   - Suggest start ที่ 50% target → ตรวจ Q-by-Q → expand ถ้า thesis confirmed

3. **Buffett block 25%** — เลือกผิดตัว = ความเสียหาย concentrated
   - CPALL/BDMS/AOT/HMPRO เป็น Thai market consensus quality
   - แต่ "consensus" = อาจ overprice + future return ลดลง
   - **Suggest:** DCA Buffett block ช้ากว่า Niwes (เพราะ valuation premium)

4. **ระหว่าง 10-15 ปีนี้** — Thai economy + global macro มี cycle (recession 2026-2028 possible per IMF, secular change China)
   - DCA mechanism จะช่วย average cost — แต่ aren't immune to permanent loss
   - **Drawdown 30-50% interim ต้องรับได้** (เคยเกิด 1997, 2008, 2020)

5. **Diversification ระหว่าง stock = สำคัญ — แต่ asset class diversification ก็ดี**
   - 100% equity (stock + cash) = aggressive
   - Consider เพิ่ม REIT 5-10%, gold 5%, foreign equity 10-15% สำหรับ true diversification
   - **กรอบนี้ assume user มี emergency fund + insurance + property แยกออกไปแล้ว**
