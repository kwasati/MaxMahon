## 1. Business Model

**Revenue 3 streams:**
1. **Land sale** (50-60% of revenue, lumpy) — sell ที่ดิน industrial zone ให้โรงงาน (Japanese, Chinese, Korean OEM)
2. **Utility income** (recurring 25-30%) — ไฟฟ้า + น้ำ + waste management + telecom + steam → factories pay ongoing
3. **Rental** (10-15%) — ready-built factory rental + warehouse rental

**Why utility income is gold:**
- Long-term contracts (20-30y)
- Sticky (factory ย้ายไม่ได้ — capital invested in machinery)
- Inflation-linked pricing
- Margin 30-40% (after infrastructure capex amortized)

**AMATA's 3 zones:**
- **AMATA City Chonburi** (Thailand EEC, 41,000+ rai) — mature, premium location, ใกล้ Laem Chabang port
- **AMATA City Rayong** (Thailand EEC, 30,000+ rai) — newer, BOI tax incentive zone
- **AMATA Smart City Bien Hoa + Long Thanh** (Vietnam, 1,300+ ha = ~8,000 ไร่) — Vietnam expansion 2010+, growing
- Total **~70,000+ rai land bank** in Thailand + Vietnam
