## 1. Business Model

**Revenue mix (banking ไทย):**
- Interest income (loan portfolio) — main driver, sensitive ดอกเบี้ย policy
- Fee + commission income (wealth, FX, transactional)
- Trading income (proprietary book — small)
- Investment income (bond + equity portfolio)

**BBL distinguish:**
- **Largest corporate + SME loan book ในไทย** — relationship banking 80+ ปี กับ Thai conglomerates (CP, ThaiBev, PTT group, etc.)
- **Overseas branch network 14 economies** — Japan (Tokyo), HK, Singapore, Indonesia (Permata Bank — full subsidiary 2020), Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, Myanmar, Malaysia, Philippines, China (Shanghai/Beijing/Xiamen), Taiwan, UK (London), USA (New York)
- **Permata Bank acquisition 2020** = ขยาย retail + SME ใน Indonesia (Top-10 bank Indonesia) — kicker สำหรับ ASEAN exposure ระยะยาว
